About HelioAPI

Making solar environmental impact transparent, measurable, and meaningful for every solar installation worldwide

Our Mission

Every solar panel installed makes a difference. Whether it's a residential, commercial or industrial system, each kilowatt-hour generated reduces our reliance on fossil fuels and cuts carbon emissions.

But here's the challenge: how much of a difference does it actually make?

The answer depends entirely on where you are. A solar system in coal-dependent Poland offsets far more CO2 than the same panel in hydropower-rich Norway. Context matters‚Äîand that's where HelioAPI comes in.

"We believe every solar user from homeowners to manufacturers deserve to know their precise environmental impact based on their location's unique energy grid. HelioAPI makes that possible."

HelioAPI provides inverter manufacturers, installers, and developers with accurate, region-specific emission factors and impact calculations. We turn raw energy data into meaningful impact equivalents that customers understand such as trees planted, miles avoided and homes powered.
